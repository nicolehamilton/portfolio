About

Education

I studied Fine Arts at Cornell University, and was obsessed with the intersection of design, communication, and information science. My favorite assignments were analyzing studies of products and how they affect users. For example, this study where it was discovered that the more aware a user is of their Facebook account, the more psychological distress they experience.

Career

I began my career through internships in design and product management roles to find what I liked best. My first experience was designing logos and websites as a Design Intern at Sports Illustrated. Next, I interned at eBay as a Product Management and Design Intern, managing and designing a B2B portal for eBay’s largest sellers. After, I interned at Organic Inc. creating advertising campaigns.

Upon graduating, I started at AOL and quickly went from designing small modules to creating entire, cross-platform experiences from flows to specs. At Tumblr, I led the UX & UI design for multiple new, top-priority features. Right now I’m leading design for Sign Up, Login, Cancellation, Ecommerce, and Workshop Finder at WW (formerly Weight Watchers).
